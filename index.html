Move a robot

To move your robot in the map, use the action move, with two options: the name of your robot, and the direction of the move: North N, South S, East E or West W. Examples: 

import json
import requests

REST_ENDPOINT = "https://robomaze.skadge.org/api"
requests.get(REST_ENDPOINT + "?move=" + json.dumps(["Wall-E", "N"]))
